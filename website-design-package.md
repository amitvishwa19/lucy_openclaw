## 2. Landing Page Structure

### Hero Section
- **Background:** Gradient from `#2563EB` to `#10B981`

- **Subheadline:** "Affordable, easy-to-use CRM for Indian hospitals & clinics. With WhatsApp integration."
- **CTA Buttons:**

- **Trust badges:** "Free 30-day trial", "No credit card required", "24x7 support"

1. **Patient Management**

2. **Appointment Scheduling**
   - Icon: calendar
   - Title: Smart Scheduling
   - Description: Online booking, doctor calendars, WhatsApp reminders

3. **Billing & GST**
   - Icon: rupee
   - Title: Easy Billing
   - Description: GST-compliant invoices, insurance claims, multi-payment gateway

4. **Pharmacy & Inventory**
   - Icon: pill
   - Title: Pharmacy Control
   - Description: Stock tracking, expiry alerts, auto-reorder

5. **Reports & Analytics**
   - Icon: chart-bar
   - Title: Insights
   - Description: Daily revenue, occupancy, doctor performance dashboards

6. **WhatsApp Integration**
   - Icon: whatsapp (use brand color)
   - Title: WhatsApp Business
   - Description: Send reminders, OTPs, payment links via WhatsApp
