## Key Tips for WhatsApp Campaign:

1. **Personalize** with doctor's name & clinic if possible
2. **Send early morning** (9-11 AM) or **evening** (4-6 PM) for better response
3. **Wait 2-3 days** between messages
4. **Use status updates** (videos, screenshots) for social proof
5. **Offer a free trial** — no commitment
6. **Include your logo** in images (if sending media)
7. **Track responses** in a spreadsheet
